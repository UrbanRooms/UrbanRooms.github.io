Urban Bliss - 272 Gloucester St

Urban Bliss is a new building designed to provide comfortable living with an open plan kitchen, living, and dining area. With WiFi broadband Internet, smart TV, and the standard modern kitchen appliances Urban Bliss has everthing you need for a great stay. Located on Gloucester Street in the heart of Christchurch it is a short walk to the central Cathedral Square and close to local shops and amenities. All bedrooms have double beds and built in wardrobes.

Urban Bliss is ideal for people who choose to live in a smaller household. The cosy cottage has a large back garden with lots of trees.

Urban Bliss has off street parking for three cars, plus additional parking on the street. Two minutes walk up the road takes you to a small convenience store and just five minutes easy walk takes you to Cathedral Square.
