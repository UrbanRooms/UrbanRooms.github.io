9. Urban Dream - 227 Worcester St

Urban Dream is a two storey renovated villa located on the corner of Worcester and Barbadoes Streets, across the road from Urban Paradise. It is a cosy family home with six bedrooms, two bathrooms, lounge and country style kitchen/dining.

Most of the bedrooms are good sized doubles and all are provided with free broadband Internet. The lounge has a comfortable sofa and chairs, coffee table, heat pump, DVD player and television for a warm night of relaxing. The fully equipped kitchen is also an excellent place for socialising and for shared meals at the cosy dining table.

There is off street parking for up to three cars and a bus stop next to the house.

A short six minute walk to Cathedral Square.
