10. Urban Apartments - 434 Cashel St

The Urban Apartments are a block of four 2-bedroom self contained apartments with everything you need to enjoy your stay in Christchurch. Each apartment is fully furnished with beds & bedding, TV, DVD, radio, lounge and dining furniture, a fully equipped kitchen, a washing machine, and free internet connection.

There are a range of local shops, including a small supermarket and ATM, just around the corner. The apartments have off street parking and a bus stop outside. The heart of Christchurch city is a short 15 minute walk away.

The Urban Apartments are perfect for a peolpe who prefer to live in a smaller house. The cost is $175 per week for a couple, or $125 per week for a single person. A free internet connection is provided, and the residents share the electricty costs using a power card, so there are no connection fees to worry about. A telephone connection is not provided.
