6. Urban Star - 283 Barbadoes St

Urban Star is a large renovated wooden building containing three independant apartments, with offstreat parking for up to five cars and the city center just around the corner Urban Star is perfect for couples and groups of friends wanting their own space in the city centre. All of the apartments come with free broadband internet, rubbish collection and gardener included. Power is paid for by the resisdents, so the listed rates are discounted by $20 to allow for this. Note that no telephone is provided at Urban Star.

Unit 1 has two large bedrooms with TVs, a hall with breakfast table, a fully equiped kitchen, and a bathroom with shower over bath and a washing machine.

Unit 2 has a large furnished lounge that can be used as a shared dormatory for friends, a good sized double bedroom with french doors opening onto a private patio, a fully equiped kitchen with washing machine, and a bathroom with a shower over bath.

Unit 3 has a combined living & dining room with sofa, DVD, TV, and dining table, a double bedroom with french doors onto a private patio, a fully equiped kitchen, bathroom and washing machine.
