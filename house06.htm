Urban Eden - 296 Barbadoes St

Urban Eden is a stylishly renovated inner city villa with five bedrooms and a cute caravan sleep-out. There is broadband internet in each bedroom. The house greets residents with a pretty front garden and a traditional front villa veranda.

Central to the home is a warm open plan kitchen, dining and living area with television and DVD player. There is a modern bathroom and separate toilet, plus one bedroom has a private bathroom. Urban Eden is the ideal place to really call home. The quality fittings, large comfortable rooms and nice shared facilities make it a place you could stay for some time.

French doors lead from the kitchen onto a large sunny transparent covered veranda with comfortable seating for socialising and relaxing outside, rain or shine. The rear garden and lawn are shared with neighbouring Urban Sunshine and Urban Haven with a shared picnic table and BBQ. Off street parking is available for one car, with additional parking on the street.

With just a short five minute walk to Cathedral Square, Urban Eden is great for city living.
