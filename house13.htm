Urban Legend - 212 Worcester St

Urban Legend meets up to its name by being a unique two-story triple brick design which are rare in Christchurch. Inside the house has been elegantly renovated to provide a friendly downstairs living, dining and kitchen area with DVD, TV, and shared telephone. The house has 4 bedrooms inside and two comfortable sleepouts with everything you need to enjoy your stay in Christchurch. Each bedroom is fully furnished with beds, bedding, furniture and wireless broadband internet. There is a fully equipped kitchen to share with your lovely house mates, leading off this is a wash room with washing machine and extra toilet. The lovely bathroom is upstairs with seperate toilet. Two of the upstairs bedrooms are very special with their own north facing decks overlooking Worcester St

The heart of Christchurch city is a short 5 minute walk away, and there are small shops located one block away.
